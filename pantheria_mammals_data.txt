                    <h3>What is <i>Ecological Archives</i>?</h3>
                    <p>For the years 1981 through 2015, <i>Ecological Archives</i> published materials supplemental to articles that appeared in the ESA journals (<i>Ecology</i>, <i>Ecological Applications</i>, <i>Ecological Monographs</i>, <i>Ecosphere</i>, <i> Ecosystem Health and Sustainability</i> and <i>Bulletin of the Ecological Society of America</i>), as well as peer-reviewed Data Papers with abstracts published in the printed journals. <i>Ecological Archives</i> is published in digital, Internet-accessible form.</p>

                                       <blockquote>
										   <p><i>Ecological Archives</i> are also hosted on FigShare. </p>
                                      <p>As of 2016, supplemental material and Data Papers are hosted at <a href="https://esajournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/">https://esajournals.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/</a> along with the journals.</p>
